Once upon a time, in a little town called Townsville, Professor Utonium was trying to create the "perfect little girl" using a mixture of "sugar, spice, and everything nice". However, he accidentally spilled a mysterious substance called "Chemical X" into the mixture.

Thus, The Powerpuff Girls were born, starring Blossom, Bubbles, and Buttercup.

All three girls have superpowers including wingless flight, super strength, superhuman speed, near invulnerability, x-ray vision, superhuman senses, red heat vision, pink-green-and-blue energy projection, invisibility, and control over lightning and fire.

Blossom is the commander and leader. Bubbles, she is the joy and the laughter. Buttercup, she is the toughest fighter.
